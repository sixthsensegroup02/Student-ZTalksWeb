EP 02: The Live Stream Data Analytics & Analysis

This documentary post revolves around the analytics of the re-livestream episode of Student-Z Talks, entitled: "Collaborative Learning: Tips and Guides." In analyzing the data, the team used the platforms' automatic analytics, which is our Facebook Page and the Streaming Feedback Form answered by the viewers as an evaluation of the stream.

EP 02: The PechaKucha Presentation

This presentation consists of the presentation of the changes Student-Z Talks has applied to their second stream, in line with the suggestions and feedback of the viewers in the first stream. Aside from that, this covers the impacts of those changes in the overall stream. The presentation has a total of 20 slides and is explained per slide ia a span of 20 seconds.
